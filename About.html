About Us

Driven by the desire for client satisfaction

Jupiter Construction

Culture and Values

Jupiter Construction is a privately owned business that has strong culture and values, to create and deliver an amazing service.

Our business is driven by the desire for client satisfaction which has been achieved by focusing on the quality of all aspects of the construction process from general advice on budget costs, planning, and the design through to the on-site construction and hand over.

Our project management team are experienced, qualified professionals who are members of the Chartered Institute of Building (CIOB).

OUR BUSINESS

How we work

Our professional approach centres on adopting a logical, client focused method of working, offering options from thinking out of the box and delivering solutions to potential problems.

We consider teamwork is fundamental to delivering a successful project and our approach is to work closely with both our clients and the design teams, with constant communication, to ensure that the project is completed on schedule, within budget and to the clients exacting specification.

OUR SERVICES

What we can do for you

Having developed long-standing and trusted relationships with many subcontractors and suppliers during our time in the industry, Jupiter Construction has a healthy and reliable supply chain. The success of any project depends on the availability of efficient resources to deliver in terms of time, cost and quality and is key to building the reputation that Jupiter Construction is striving for.

As a client of Jupiter Construction you can expect the highest level of service whether your project be a new build, refurbishment or management contracting. Our meticulous approach lends naturally to seamless transition during the life-cycle of any project. Whilst our approach of overlapping phases dramatically reduces project time.

OUR EXPERIENCE

A professional team with over 25 years experience

Whether working on traditional or design and build, our ability to provide a flexible and personal approach to our clients expectations generates a high percentage of repeat business.

The portfolio of our experienced team contains new build developments, extensions, conversions and refurbishments. Our construction renovation and fit-out work is renowned for being meticulously planned and immaculately executed.

JOSHOB is a diversified construction company, made up of teams of people who are proven in their industries. All working to build and maintain projects all over Ghana.
